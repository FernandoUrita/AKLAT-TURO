About AKLAT-TURO

Discover our mission to preserve and share Filipino literary heritage

Our Story

AKLAT-TURO was founded in 2025 with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a digital sanctuary for Filipino literature where classic and contemporary stories can be preserved, shared, and celebrated by readers of all ages.

Our platform was born from a passion for Philippine literature and a desire to make these important cultural works more accessible to both Filipino readers and those interested in learning about our rich literary tradition. We believe that stories are not just entertainment, but vital connections to our history, values, and identity as a people.

The name "AKLAT-TURO" combines the Filipino words for "book" (aklat) and "teach" (turo), reflecting our dual purpose of being both a library of stories and an educational resource.

Preservation

We digitally preserve important works of Filipino literature to ensure they remain accessible for future generations.

Education

We provide educational resources to help students and teachers explore Philippine literature more deeply.

Community

We foster a community of readers and writers passionate about Filipino stories and culture.
